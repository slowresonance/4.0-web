Frequently Asked Questions
How is Ecficio pronounced?
“Ec-fee-cho”
Is Ecficio part of Convergence?
No.
What do you mean by Ecficio is Hybrid?
Ecficio 4.0H has a mixture of online and offline events, competitions and talks.
What are the prerequisites?
There are no prerequisites.
How can I register for the fest?
Approach us at our registration desks (infront of B-block and Hungry Jacks) to proceed with the payment.
What is the Entry Fee?
The entry fee is Rs 300 if you register alone. There is a discount of Rs 50 if you register as a group (So the ticket costs Rs 250).
